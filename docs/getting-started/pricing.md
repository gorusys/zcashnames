# Pricing

Claim costs are tiered by name length. Shorter names cost more because they're scarcer.

| Name Length | Cost (ZEC) | Cost (Zatoshis) |
|-------------|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 character | 6.00 | 600,000,000 |
| 2 characters | 4.25 | 425,000,000 |
| 3 characters | 3.00 | 300,000,000 |
| 4 characters | 1.50 | 150,000,000 |
| 5 characters | 0.75 | 75,000,000 |
| 6 characters | 0.50 | 50,000,000 |
| 7+ characters | 0.25 | 25,000,000 |

> 1 ZEC = 100,000,000 zatoshis

## Examples

| Name | Length | Cost |
|------|--------|------|
| `z` | 1 | 6.00 ZEC |
| `hi` | 2 | 4.25 ZEC |
| `bob` | 3 | 3.00 ZEC |
| `alice` | 5 | 0.75 ZEC |
| `zcashfan` | 8 | 0.25 ZEC |

## Marketplace pricing

Names listed for sale have their own prices set by the seller. When you buy a name, you pay the listed price (not the claim cost). The claim cost only applies to unclaimed names.

## USD estimates

The search interface shows approximate USD equivalents based on the current ZEC/USD exchange rate, fetched from Coinbase, CoinGecko, and Kraken (with fallback).
