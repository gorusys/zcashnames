Zcash addresses are long, ugly, and impossible to remember.

Claim a name like `julian.zcash`, and anyone can resolve it to your shielded address. Everything happens on-chain through [ZIP-321](https://zips.z.cash/zip-0321) payment URIs and signed memos.
