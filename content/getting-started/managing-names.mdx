# Managing Names

Once you own a name, you can update the address it points to. All management actions require [OTP verification](otp-verification) to prove you control the registered address.

## Updating your address

If you rotate your wallet keys or want the name to point somewhere else:

1. Search for your registered name
2. Click **Update Address**
3. Complete OTP verification
4. Enter the new Zcash address
5. Send the transaction

The name now resolves to your new address. The old address is freed up.

## How OTP verification fits in

Every management action (update, list, delist) requires you to prove you own the address the name is registered to. You do this by sending yourself a small verification transaction and entering a 6-digit code. See [OTP Verification](otp-verification) for the details.
