# Buying & Selling Names

ZNS has a built-in marketplace. If someone owns a name, they can list it for sale. If a name is listed, anyone can buy it.

## Buying a name

1. Search for a name that shows as **For Sale**
2. Enter your Zcash address
3. The app generates a payment link for the listed price
4. Send the transaction from your wallet

Once confirmed, the name transfers to you. The previous owner loses it — there's no escrow or approval step. It's instant and final.

## Listing a name for sale

If you own a name, you can put it on the marketplace at any price you choose.

1. Search for your registered name
2. Click **List for Sale**
3. Complete [OTP verification](otp-verification) to prove you own the address
4. Set your price in ZEC
5. Send the transaction

Your name shows up in the marketplace and anyone can buy it. You can change the price by listing again — the new price replaces the old one.

## Delisting

Changed your mind? You can remove your name from the marketplace at any time. It stays registered to you, just no longer for sale.

1. Search for your listed name
2. Click **Delist**
3. Complete [OTP verification](otp-verification)
4. Send the transaction

## Price limits

- Minimum: 0.001 ZEC
- Maximum: 21,000,000 ZEC (the Zcash supply cap)
